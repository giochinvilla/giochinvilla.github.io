Map

This is the campaign global map. Your adventure will see you travel between the continent of Waves. Numerous kingdoms exist within this continent. Each has its own story, its own politics of its secrets and its plots. Centered by the large tower at its center and surrounded by peaks that cannot be reached like a rose, the realms of mortals thrive. There are numerous breeds that have come to this continent in the hope of finding success.

Zoom: 100%

The Kingdoms

Currently there are 17 kingdoms on the continent. They are scattered and varied. There are the elven realms immersed in the deepest forests. The kingdom in the flying islands is the most developed kingdom, their technological researches are the most advanced, but they would be nothing without the enormous quality of materials coming from the dwarven kingdom. Dragonborns and tieflings prefer the mild east sea, the most prosperous for fishing. It is said that in the north, someone still lives in the shadow of the tower. But few have ever returned from that area.
